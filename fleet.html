Q400

The Bombardier Q400 is a regional turboprop aircraft used for short-haul flights, perfect for flying to smaller airports while maintaining high efficiency and speed.

B737-700

The Boeing 737-700 is a popular single-aisle airliner that offers a great balance of performance and capacity, ideal for medium to short-haul flights.

B737-800

The Boeing 737-800 is an advanced version of the 737 series, offering increased efficiency and range for medium-haul flights, commonly used in many major airlines.

B737-8MAX

The Boeing 737 MAX 8 brings advanced aerodynamics, larger engines, and new winglets, allowing it to fly more efficiently while maintaining the same seating capacity.

B787-9

The Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner is a long-range, wide-body aircraft that offers a smooth and comfortable flight experience on intercontinental flights, with impressive fuel efficiency.
