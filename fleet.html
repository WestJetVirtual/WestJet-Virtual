Our Fleet

Q400
The Q400 is a versatile turboprop aircraft, ideal for regional flights. It offers efficiency and speed, with a comfortable ride for passengers. Perfect for quick hops between cities.
B737-700
The Boeing 737-700 is a workhorse of the skies, offering efficient medium-haul capabilities. With seating for over 140 passengers, it's perfect for both domestic and international flights.
B737-800
The Boeing 737-800 provides a combination of capacity and efficiency. This aircraft serves routes of varying lengths, making it a reliable choice for short and medium-haul flights.
B737-8MAX
The 737-8MAX offers cutting-edge fuel efficiency, a quieter cabin, and greater range. It is designed to meet the growing demand for more efficient air travel while ensuring passenger comfort.
B787-9
The Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner is known for its long-range capabilities and unparalleled passenger comfort. It offers larger windows, improved air quality, and reduced fuel consumption.
