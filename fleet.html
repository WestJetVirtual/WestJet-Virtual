Our Fleet

Q400

The Bombardier Q400 is a turboprop aircraft known for its speed and fuel efficiency. It's ideal for short regional routes, combining comfort and performance for our virtual pilots.

B737-700

The Boeing 737-700 is a versatile narrow-body aircraft that offers a great balance of range and capacity. Perfect for medium-haul routes, it's a favorite for virtual pilots seeking efficiency.

B737-800

The Boeing 737-800 is one of the most popular airliners in the world, offering both performance and comfort. With a larger capacity than the 737-700, it's perfect for medium to long-haul routes.

B737-8MAX

The Boeing 737 MAX 8 is a more fuel-efficient version of the 737, offering longer range and enhanced performance. Ideal for longer flights, it's the modern choice for WestJet Virtual pilots.

B787-9

The Boeing 787-9 is a wide-body, long-haul aircraft known for its advanced technology, fuel efficiency, and passenger comfort. This aircraft is perfect for virtual international routes, providing a smooth and modern flying experience.
