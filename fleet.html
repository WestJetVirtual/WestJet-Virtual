Our Fleet

At WestJet Virtual, we pride ourselves on our diverse fleet. Our aircraft range from the sleek Q400 to the long-haul B787-9, ensuring that we are ready for any route and destination. Here's a look at our fleet:

Q400

The Bombardier Q400 is a versatile regional aircraft, perfect for short-haul routes with high efficiency.

B737-700

The Boeing 737-700 is a reliable workhorse, offering both comfort and fuel efficiency on mid-range routes.

B737-800

The Boeing 737-800 is a popular choice for airlines worldwide, offering excellent range and passenger comfort.

B737-8MAX

The Boeing 737 MAX 8 brings next-generation efficiency and performance to our fleet, perfect for short to medium-haul routes.

B787-9

The Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner is designed for long-haul flights, offering the ultimate in comfort and efficiency.
