Welcome to WestJet Virtual

Welcome to WestJet Virtual, a premier virtual airline within the Infinite Flight community, dedicated to delivering an exceptional flying experience that mirrors the excellence of WestJet Airlines. Our mission is to provide pilots with a professional and immersive environment, fostering a community of aviation enthusiasts who share a passion for virtual flight.

Our fleet consists of modern aircraft, including the Boeing 737-800, designed for efficiency and passenger comfort. Explore more about our aircraft on the official WestJet website.
