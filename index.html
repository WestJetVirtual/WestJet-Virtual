About Us

WestJet Virtual is a community-driven virtual airline for flight simulation enthusiasts. We strive to provide a realistic and fun experience, with a professional atmosphere for all our pilots. Whether you are a seasoned flight simulator user or just starting out, we welcome you to join us on our virtual flights!
