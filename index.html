About Us

WestJet Virtual is a flight simulation group in Infinite Flight, dedicated to replicating the operations of WestJet Airlines. We offer a professional, friendly, and supportive environment for all aviation enthusiasts. Whether you're new to flying or a seasoned pro, we strive to bring an exciting and educational experience to our members. Our goal is to create a realistic virtual experience while fostering a fun and engaging community.
