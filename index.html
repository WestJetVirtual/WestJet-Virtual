About Us

WestJet Virtual is a passionate community of flight simulation enthusiasts in Infinite Flight. We strive to emulate the operations of WestJet Airlines, offering a variety of routes, aircraft, and events for our members to enjoy. Whether you're flying short regional routes or embarking on long-haul flights, there's always a seat for you at WestJet Virtual.
