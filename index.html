About Us

WestJet Virtual is a passionate community of flight simulation enthusiasts inspired by the real-world WestJet Airlines. Operating within Infinite Flight, we bring the joy of flying to virtual skies with a focus on realism, camaraderie, and fun. Whether you're a seasoned pilot or just starting out, join us to explore stunning destinations, fly a diverse fleet, and experience the thrill of aviation—all with real-world weather conditions.
