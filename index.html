About Us

WestJet Virtual is a flight simulation community that replicates the operations of the real-world WestJet Airlines, offering an exciting and realistic experience for pilots in Infinite Flight. Our team is committed to creating a fun and professional virtual airline where all members can enjoy the thrill of virtual aviation.
