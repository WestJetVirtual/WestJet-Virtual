About Us

WestJet Virtual is a flight simulation community based on the real-world WestJet Airlines. Our goal is to provide a fun, engaging, and realistic flying experience for Infinite Flight pilots across the globe. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just beginning your journey in virtual aviation, WestJet Virtual offers the tools, community, and support to help you take off.
