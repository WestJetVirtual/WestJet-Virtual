Join WestJet Virtual

We are excited to welcome passionate pilots to WestJet Virtual. Please ensure you meet the following requirements before applying:

  • Must be at least 15 years of age
  • Must have a valid IFC account
  • Must have a valid copy of Infinite Flight Pro
  • Must not be on the IFVARB blacklist
  • Must be in good standing on the IFC
  • Must have a Discord account and be willing to join our Discord server
  • Must be at least Grade 3

If you meet all of the above requirements, we look forward to having you join our team at WestJet Virtual!
